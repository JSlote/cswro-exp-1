Lag measurement

In order to measure how much lag there is in your keyboard input, we would like you to keep the beat to a song using the spacebar. Keep time with the song by pressing the spacebar down, then up, then down, and so on, each beat. Use the visual guide to help you.


Click "start" to practice. Keep going until the music stops.

Troubleshooting Your Sound

There are a couple things you should check on:
Internet Connection
Is the Captcha still spinning? You may have lost your internet connection.
Speakers
Is your system volume and speaker volume set correctly? Is anything on mute?
Adobe Flash Player
Do you have Flash Player installed?
